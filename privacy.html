MailChief Privacy Policy Last updated: May 27, 2025 1. Introduction MailChief ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MailChief email marketing platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your rights in relation to your personal data. 2. Information We Collect Personal Information You Provide: Account details: name, email address, phone number, company name, billing address. Authentication credentials: password, OAuth tokens for connected services (e.g., Google, Shopify). Content data: uploaded contact lists, email templates, campaign content. Automatically Collected Information: Usage data: page views, feature interactions, clickstream, error logs. Device & connection data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers. Analytics data: aggregated logs, performance metrics. 3. How We Use Your Information Service Provision: to deliver, maintain, and improve MailChief features. Authentication & Authorization: to authenticate your account; to access third-party APIs under explicit consent. Communication: to send account notices, security alerts, and support messages. Personalization & Optimization: to tailor your experience and recommend features. Legal Compliance: to fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure Service Providers: third-party vendors (hosting, payment processors, email delivery). Integrations: minimal data exchange with connected services via OAuth2 scopes. Legal Requirements: disclosure under lawful requests, court orders, or to protect rights and safety. Business Transfers: in events like mergers or acquisitions, data may transfer under confidentiality terms. 5. Data Retention We retain data while your account is active or as required by law. Deleted accounts are purged from active systems; backups may persist up to 60 days. 6. Your Rights Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@mailchief.com. 7. Security We implement technical and organizational safeguards (TLS, encryption at rest, access controls, regular audits) to protect your data. 8. Children's Privacy Not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact us to remove any such data.
